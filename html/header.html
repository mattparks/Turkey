Turkey Intro

The history of the Turkish can be broken into the medieval history of the Seljuk Empire, the Ottoman Empire, and the Republic of Turkey. Just like with Russians, Turkish people have always had a authoritarian leader throughout the three major governments. Atatürk was the greatest leader of Turkey, he pushed for a secular government and industrialized the nation. The modern secular Republic of Turkey was created after the overthrow of the Sultan in 1922. Atatürks power created a single-party state from 1923 to 1938. 4 major coups were launched to ensure secularism and create order in the Republic.

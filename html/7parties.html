Political parties / Election systems and recent results

Parliment elections are done though a proportional representaton (PR) system. There are 85 districts, electing 550 total members to the Parliament. Presidental elections are run like Iran with a two-round runoff system, if one candadate does not get 50%+1 of the vote; a second round of the top two candadates will run.

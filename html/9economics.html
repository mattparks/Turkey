Economic system/policy (current)

Turkey like much of the west uses a capitalist open economy, they have much open trade with Europe and other nation such as the US and China. Turkey uses the Lira as there currency, where $1 is 3.51 Lira, there GDP is 718.2 bullion USD as of 2015. Unemployment is at 8% in 2012, the US was 4.7% in 2016. Unlike the UK and Russia, Turkey has a very small amount of state owned businesses as they have been liberalised throughout the 20th and 21st centuries.
